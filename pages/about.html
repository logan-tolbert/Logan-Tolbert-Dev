About Me

Hello! I'm Logan, a dedicated full stack software developer from Birmingham, AL. My journey into tech started after a decade-long career as a mechanic. Though I had no prior experience in programming, I felt a strong desire to better myself and pursue a field that truly excited me.

In January 2024, I took the leap and quit my job to focus on personal growth and explore the world of software development. I began with self-learning, diving into online courses and resources. While I initially experimented with HTML, CSS, JavaScript my curiosity for deeper programming led me to Python. Despite hitting a wall with Object-Oriented Programming concepts, I didn't give up.

Determined to overcome my challenges, I joined a local bootcamp that taught C#. This experience has been transformative, and I've made considerable progress. I'm currently working on my final project for graduation, which has been a rewarding and eye-opening journey.

While I may not have an extensive portfolio or decades of experience in tech, my diverse background and relentless drive to learn and grow are my biggest strengths. I've completed numerous mini-projects, each contributing to my understanding and skills.

When I'm not coding, you can find my playing guitar, listening to music, or gaming. I'm passionate about continuous learning and always eager to tackle new challenges and push the boundaries of what's possible.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to connecting and exploring opportunities to collaborate!
